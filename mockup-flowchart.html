Pasta Carbonara

Main Dishes
⏲ Prep: 10 min 🍳 Cook: 20 min 🍵 Serves: 4
Ingredients
Spaghetti 400 g
Guanciale 200 g
Egg yolks 4
Pecorino Romano 100 g
Black pepper to taste
Steps
Each step is a card connected by arrows — like a cooking pipeline
1
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
2
Cut the guanciale into small strips and fry in a dry pan until crispy
3
Mix egg yolks with grated pecorino and plenty of black pepper
4
Cook spaghetti until al dente, reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining
5
Toss hot pasta into the guanciale pan (heat OFF), then quickly stir in the egg mixture
6
Add pasta water a splash at a time until creamy. Serve immediately with extra pecorino

Design Notes

  • Vertical flow — reads top to bottom like a timeline
  • Connected nodes — orange arrows link each step to the next
  • Numbered circles — quick visual anchor for where you are
  • Tap highlight — hover/active state (orange border) so you can track your current step while cooking
  • Mobile-first — sized for phone, scrolls naturally
  • Ingredients up top — check what you need before you start the flow
